Look at the sky at night. We can see stars in the sky. And we can see some patterns in the star.

Some patterns look like people. Other patterns look like animals. These patterns are constellations.

We can't always see the same constellations. They change from season to season. Why?

The earth spins. It also goes around the sun. And some stars go behind the sun. Then we can't see them from the earth. So we see different constellations each season.
